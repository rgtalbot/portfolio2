About Me

Hello! Thanks for stopping by my site. Before you look around, let me tell you a little about myself. I have spent the past 4 years as an independent financial advisor with Raymond James. Recently, I decided to give it all up and follow something I was more passionate about. As soon as I discovered the world of programming, I instantly fell in love. I have so much fun writing even the most simple of code and watching it work. And that is where I am today. Attending the UCF Coding Bootcamp and using every resource I can get my hands on to consume as much knowledge as possible. I am looking forward to becoming a full stack web developer and building and fixing things for people every day.

In addition to learning to code, my other hobbies include golf, running, serving at my church, listening to podcasts and spending time with my wife. We have been married for 3 years and while we do not have any kids yet, we do have a 2 year old black labrador named Miles who keeps us both plenty busy. Some of my favorite podcasts include 'Wait Wait ... Don't Tell Me!', Developer Tea, MarketFoolery, JavascriptJabber, Stuff You Should Know, The Bill Simmons Podcast, This American Life, and Learn to Code With Me.
